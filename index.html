About Me

Hello! My name is Aaron Lu and I am an aerospace engineer and software developer with experience in control theory and blockchain development.

My fascination is with complex systems - understanding them, developing them, and ultimately, improving them. Through my studies and work experience, I realized that the foundation of many complex systems is code - from control systems to websites, everything involves code!

In my spare time, I love being outdoors and working with my hands. It can look like anything from hiking to ultimate frisbee to cooking to random projects!

From aerospace control systems to blockchain protocol integrations, I am fascinated with understanding, developing, and, ultimately, improving complex systems.

Toronto: From suburban Michigan to a city of 2.8 million.

Hiking: There's something so peaceful and satisfying to sit at the peak of a trail.

Ultimate Frisbee: My favorite urban activity - I'm always up for tossing a frisbee around.

Cooking: It's like a really tasty experiment!

Projects: Because learning never stops!
